Our Mission

At Spirit of M46 Rescue, we are driven by a deep passion for providing a second chance to neglected, abandoned, and mistreated horses. Our mission is to rescue these beautiful animals from harmful situations, whether due to abuse, neglect, or abandonment, and offer them a safe space to heal, recover, and thrive.

We understand that every horse's needs are unique, which is why we offer individualized care, including medical treatment, nutritional support, and mental rehabilitation. Our dedicated team works closely with veterinarians and equine specialists to create personalized recovery plans, ensuring that each horse receives the attention and compassion it deserves. Beyond recovery, our goal is to find these horses loving forever homes, where they can continue to thrive and live peacefully.

Horses that are unable to be rehomed due to special medical or behavioral needs are given a permanent sanctuary at our facility, where they can live out their days in a serene and nurturing environment.

Our Services

We offer a variety of services to support horse welfare and rehabilitation:

  • Equine Rescue: Our primary focus is rescuing horses from dangerous, abusive, or neglectful situations. We work closely with local authorities and other rescue organizations to ensure these horses are brought to safety.
  • Rehabilitation: Every horse we rescue receives a customized rehabilitation plan, which includes veterinary care, dietary improvements, physical therapy, and socialization. Whether a horse requires medical treatment for injuries or emotional support from past trauma, we are here to guide them through the healing process.
  • Adoption: Once a horse is rehabilitated, we work tirelessly to find them a loving home. Our adoption process is thorough, ensuring that the horses are matched with the right families or individuals who can meet their ongoing needs.
  • Sanctuary: For horses that cannot be adopted due to chronic health conditions or other limitations, we offer a permanent sanctuary. These horses receive lifelong care at our facility, enjoying open pastures and companionship from other sanctuary horses.
  • Pasture Board: We provide open, spacious pastures where horses can roam freely, graze, and socialize. Our pasture boarding services are ideal for owners looking to give their horses a natural and enriching environment.
  • Stall Board: Our clean, comfortable stables provide individual stalls with high-quality bedding, ensuring the horses are well-rested and safe. Stall boarding includes daily exercise and pasture access.
  • On-site Trailer Repair: Keeping horses safe during transport is essential, which is why we offer on-site trailer repair services. From regular maintenance to emergency fixes, our professional repair services ensure safe travels for all horses.

How You Can Help

You can help by adopting, volunteering, or donating to support our cause. Every contribution goes directly to the care of the horses. Visit our 'View Horses' section to meet some of the horses currently up for adoption, or contact us for more ways to get involved.

Financial support allows us to continue providing top-tier care for the horses. Donations go directly toward medical expenses, food, facility upkeep, and rescue operations. Consider making a one-time contribution or becoming a monthly donor to sustain our efforts.

Whether through adoption, volunteering, donating, or simply sharing our message, your support plays a vital role in ensuring these horses get the second chance they deserve.
